Ode to Niche Cloud Providers

In the realm where data soars and bytes abound,

Where giants once stood on computing ground,

Emerging stars now brightly gleam,

Niche cloud providers, the new supreme.


AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud,

Once reigned supreme, bold and proud,

But now, new challengers rise,

With tailored services, they mesmerize.


From healthcare to finance, they pave the way,

Security and compliance, they proudly display,

Nimble and agile, they swiftly adapt,

Innovation their creed, never to lapse.


So here's to the underdogs, the ones on the rise,

With them, the future of cloud surely lies,

Niche cloud providers, we sing your praise,

For in your hands, our data finds its blaze.
